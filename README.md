Analyze customer data from an online retail business to identify meaningful customer segments and gain insights for marketing and business strategies.

## Introduction

This project aims to perform customer segmentation analysis using data from an online retail business. Customer segmentation is a crucial step for businesses to understand their customer base, tailor marketing strategies, and improve customer satisfaction. By identifying distinct customer segments, this analysis can help in targeted marketing, product recommendations, and optimizing business operations.

## Features

- Data Preprocessing: Clean and prepare the customer data for analysis.
- Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Gain insights through data visualization and statistical analysis.
- Customer Segmentation: Use clustering algorithms to group customers based on various features.
- Insights and Recommendations: Provide actionable insights and recommendations for marketing and business strategies.
